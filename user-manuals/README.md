## Quick Start

1. Read the **Platform Overview** to understand what MAOF does
2. Follow the **Setup Guide** to get the platform running
3. Open the **Dashboard Guide** to create your account and first team
4. Follow the **Bot Integration Guide** to connect your Telegram bot
